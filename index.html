A Self-Paced Course

Wing & Airfoil Design for Long-Endurance Flight

Designing an optimal wing for a 1–2 m battery-electric drone — from first principles to XFOIL, XFLR5, and CFD.

The mission
Understand — not just copy — how wings and airfoils work, then design a near-optimal wing for maximum endurance and validate it with computer tools. The recurring north star: maximise CL1.5/CD in the low-Reynolds regime.
