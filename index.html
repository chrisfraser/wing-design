◎ The mission

Understand — not just copy — how wings and airfoils work, then design a near-optimal wing for maximum endurance and validate it with computer tools. The recurring north star: maximise CL1.5/CD in the low-Reynolds regime (~150k–400k) our small wing lives in. Glossary · Mission · Resources
