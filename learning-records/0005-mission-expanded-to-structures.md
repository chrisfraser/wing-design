# Mission expanded: structures & the physical build brought into scope

On 2026-06-27 the user asked to add a **Phase 5 on structures and the build**. This reverses an
original [[MISSION]] exclusion ("Structural/spar design, materials, and manufacturing" was out of
scope). The mission now runs all the way to a built wing, not just a designed one.

Why it matters for future sessions: the aero arc (F1–D2) optimises C_L^1.5/CD and lands on a
high-aspect-ratio wing — but high AR and thin low-Re airfoils are exactly what make a wing
structurally hard (root bending moment ∝ load × span; thin section → taller spar caps needed). So
Phase 5 isn't a tangent; it closes the loop the aero opened, and the "every gram costs endurance"
weight argument ties structures straight back to wing loading (T2) and the figure of merit.

Phase 5 lessons (codes B1–B3): B1 The Wing as a Beam (spar & loads), B2 Materials & Construction
(weight budget), B3 Build the Wing (steps + checklist). Grounded in a dedicated structures research
pass; RESOURCES.md gains a Structures & Build section.
